Unicorn

The unicorn is a legendary creature that has been described since antiquity as a beast with a single large, pointed, spiraling horn projecting from its forehead.

  • Grouping: Mythology
  • Mythological Origin: Asia
  • Habitat: Scotland
  • Abilities: Power to render poisoned water potable, Heals sickness, Has magical powers

We do not recommend killing or harming unicorns, but hunting for them can be a lot of fun. To do that you will need an official “Unicorn Hunting License”. You can get your own Unicorn Hunting License for free at the Lake Superior University. This license is good for a lifetime (and beyond) and can be used anywhere on earth.
